Why Get AWS Certified?

AWS certifications validate your cloud expertise, opening doors to top tech companies and high-paying roles. Here are some benefits:

  • Gain global recognition and career credibility.
  • Expand your technical knowledge and skills.
  • Unlock exclusive resources like AWS Summit invites and training materials.

Foundational

Beginner-level knowledge of AWS Cloud and core services.

  • AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner

Associate

In-depth knowledge of AWS services for specific roles.

  • Solutions Architect Associate
  • Developer Associate
  • SysOps Administrator Associate

Professional

Advanced skills for complex AWS architecture and operations.

  • Solutions Architect Professional
  • DevOps Engineer Professional

Specialty

Deep expertise in specialized AWS domains.

  • Advanced Networking
  • Data Analytics
  • Security
  • Machine Learning
